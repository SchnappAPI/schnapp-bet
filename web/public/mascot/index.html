Backstory

Schnappy grew up in a Louisiana bayou where every other gator lunged at whatever splashed. They went hungry a lot. Schnappy noticed something: the water tells you everything if you sit still long enough. So he waited — eyes just above the surface — and struck maybe once a day. He never missed.

One flood season a riverboat casino washed a stack of odds sheets into his swamp. He read them the way he read the water: most numbers were noise, a few were wrong, and the wrong ones were dinner. He's been grading lines ever since. The name on the door — schnapp.bet — is his: schnappen, to snap it up.

Personality

  • Patient, not passive. Default state is the lurk. No pick is a fine day.
  • Numbers-first. Talks in stats and edges, never in locks and vibes.
  • Dry humor. Deadpan one-liners; the specs do the smiling.
  • Honest with Ls. Losses get one sweat drop and a graded row, never tilt.
  • Anti-degen. He is the opposite of chase culture; that is the whole point of him.

Construction

  • Flat vector on a 240-unit canvas (full body 240×250). Transparent background.
  • Line weights: 5 outer / 3 inner / 2.5 teeth. Never black — outline is #0E3B2A.
  • Eye bumps r26 at y52 · white r15 · pupil r6.5. Cranium (120,105) rx72 ry52. Muzzle (120,126) rx48 ry27. Torso (120,188) rx46 ry38.
  • Eyes sit on top of the head (gator, not frog). Round violet specs r20 — the brand tie, never removed.
  • Shading is flat two-tone only: #22815A scutes, far limbs, cheek scale patches. No gradients, ever.
  • Two teeth in almost every mouth. Ridged tail. Mitt hands with two finger notches.

Usage rules

Do

  • Use the lurk as the signature mark; pair Schnappy with real data.
  • Keep the specs on in every frame; keep eyes on top of the head.
  • Recolor props per sport accent; the gator himself never recolors.
  • Win pose for graded winners only; losses get the sweat drop and a graded row.
  • Voice: adult, dry, numbers-first. He talks probabilities, not promises.

Don’t

  • No gradients, no black outlines, no drop shadows baked into the SVGs.
  • No hype framing — fire/lock emojis, “can’t lose”, streak-chasing.
  • Never childlike styling or kid-targeted contexts — this is an 18+ product.
  • Don’t redraw by hand — every asset regenerates from web/scripts/generate_mascot.py.
  • Don’t use the win pose, starry eyes, or confetti on ungraded picks.
