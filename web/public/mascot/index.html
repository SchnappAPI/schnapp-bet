Backstory

Schnappy grew up in a Louisiana bayou where every other gator lunged at whatever splashed. They went hungry a lot. Schnappy noticed something: the water tells you everything if you sit still long enough. So he waited — eyes just above the surface — and struck maybe once a day. He never missed.

One flood season a riverboat casino washed a stack of odds sheets into his swamp. He read them the way he read the water: most numbers were noise, a few were wrong, and the wrong ones were dinner. He's been grading lines ever since. The name on the door — schnapp.bet — is his: schnappen, to snap it up.

Personality

  • Patient, not passive. Default state is the lurk. No pick is a fine day.
  • Numbers-first. Talks in stats and edges, never in locks and vibes.
  • Dry humor. Deadpan one-liners; the specs do the smiling.
  • Honest with Ls. Losses get one sweat drop and a graded row, never tilt.
  • Anti-degen. He is the opposite of chase culture; that is the whole point of him.

Build rules

  • Flat vector, dark outline #0E3B2A at 5px on a 240-unit canvas.
  • Eyes sit on top of the head (gator, not frog) with round violet specs — the specs are the brand tie and never come off.
  • Two teeth visible in almost every mouth. Ridged tail enters from either side.
  • Transparent background; drawn for the dark UI but holds up on white.

Usage

Do

  • Use The Lurk as the signature mark — headers, empty states, “no plays today”.
  • Pair a pose with a real number (a graded pick, an EV figure). Schnappy always stands next to data.
  • Scale freely — it’s SVG. Head chips at 32–64px, full poses 160px+.
  • Recolor confetti/props to fit the sport accent; body colors stay fixed.

Don’t

  • No cash emojis, fire, or “LOCK 🔒” framing next to him — he doesn’t chase and doesn’t hype.
  • Don’t remove the glasses or flip the eye placement off the top of the head.
  • Don’t use the win pose for ungraded picks. He celebrates results, not slips.
  • Don’t outline in black or restyle with gradients; flat + dark-green line only.
