## Key decisions made this session

- Exact hits (stat == line) count as Won for both Over and Under
- Composite grade removed from display, stays in DB for historical comparison
- MODEL_VERSION for v2: `grading-v2.0`
- Market groups: Volume (PTS/PRA/PR/PA/RA), Rate (3PM/STL/BLK), Counting (REB/AST)
- Relevance weight = recency_weight × role_weight × context_weight
- Recency: exp(-0.035 × days_ago)
- Role: Gaussian σ=6 min centered on current role (avg of last 5 games)
- Context: 1.5 playoff vs same opponent, 1.2 any playoff, 1.0 regular season
- Calibration holdout: chronological split, train days 1–30, holdout days 31–37
- Profit-weighted backtest: flat $1 stake, sum(ev_pct) where model_prob > implied_prob
- KDE four-tier system replaced by EV% per posted line
- Distribution fit: negative binomial for counting stats, normal for combo stats
- Display: Model Prob + EV% in condensed view, implied prob + component breakdown on expand
- Color: EV% > 8% green, 0–8% yellow, < 0% gray
